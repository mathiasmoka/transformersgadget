Process

Transform in seconds,
not minutes.

01
Pack your gear — just like any backpack

40L of organized storage with laptop sleeve, hip-belt pockets, and hidden valuables pocket. Start your day as a regular bag-carrier.

🎒
02
Identify what you need — bag reads the moment

Rain incoming? Need to rest? Emergency shelter? Each mode has a dedicated quick-release mechanism on the outer frame.

🧠
03
Pull the trigger — one motion, full transformation

Color-coded pull tabs make each transform intuitive. No manual, no instructions needed. If you can open a bag, you can do this.

⚡
04
Lock in — structurally rigid in under 30 seconds

Magnetic locking pins confirm each position with a satisfying click. The system holds under load, wind, and weather until you decide to change again.
